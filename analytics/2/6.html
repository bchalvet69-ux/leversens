Why Correlation Matters

The correlation matrix shows how our 5 assets move relative to each other. Values close to +1 mean assets move together (e.g. GOLD & NEM), close to -1 means they move in opposite directions, and 0 means no relationship. For portfolio construction, we want low correlations between assets — this is the foundation of diversification.

Risk Management

When markets crash, correlations tend to spike — everything drops together, destroying diversification precisely when you need it most. This phenomenon is called correlation breakdown. Monitoring the stability of correlations over time helps anticipate when a diversified portfolio might suddenly act like a concentrated one, triggering the need for hedging or position reduction.
